Emotion-Based Music Recommendation

Discover music that matches your current mood! Our system will analyze your facial expressions through your webcam and recommend songs that complement your detected emotion.

How it works:
  1. Click the "Start Camera" button below
  2. Allow camera access when prompted
  3. Position yourself clearly in the frame with good lighting
  4. Look directly at the camera and keep a neutral expression initially
  5. Click "Detect My Emotion" to begin the 3-second analysis
  6. After processing, you'll see your image with face detection boxes and emotion labels
Tips for best results:
  • Ensure your face is well-lit and clearly visible
  • Remove glasses if they interfere with detection
  • Stay relatively still during the 3-second countdown
  • Position yourself 1-2 feet from the camera
